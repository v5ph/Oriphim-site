Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 7, 2026

Oriphim Labs LLC ("Oriphim", "we", "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of institutional clients. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information Collected

We collect the following data:

  • Contact Information: Email, name, company name, and phone number
  • Technical Data: IP address, API usage logs, access timestamps, and system diagnostics
  • Billing Information: Payment data handled securely via third-party processor (Stripe)
  • Usage Analytics: Signal request frequency, model versioning preferences, integration methods

2. Use of Data

We use collected information for:

  • Account creation and API access provisioning
  • Signal delivery and infrastructure maintenance
  • Technical support and troubleshooting
  • Billing, invoicing, and payment processing
  • Security monitoring and fraud prevention
  • Service improvement and performance optimization

3. Data Sharing

Oriphim does not sell, rent, or distribute client data to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data with:

  • Infrastructure Providers: Hosting services, database management (e.g., Supabase, AWS)
  • Payment Processors: Stripe for secure billing and subscription management
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to enforce our terms of service

4. Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect client data:

  • TLS encryption for all API communications
  • Secure API key rotation policies (90-day default)
  • Role-based access control and authentication
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Data isolation between client environments

5. Client Confidentiality

We treat all client information as confidential. This includes strategy names, signal access patterns, usage statistics, and integration details. Client data is never disclosed publicly or used for competitive analysis.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Oriphim uses minimal tracking for service functionality:

  • Session cookies for authenticated API access
  • Privacy-safe analytics (e.g., Plausible Analytics) for website performance monitoring
  • No third-party advertising or behavioral tracking cookies

7. Data Retention

Client data is retained for the duration of the active license plus 12 months following account closure, unless otherwise required by law or mutually agreed upon for audit purposes.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Object to processing or request data portability
  • Withdraw consent for data usage (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@oriphim.com

9. International Data Transfers

Oriphim operates primarily within the United States. If you are accessing services from outside the U.S., your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. where our infrastructure is located.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Clients will be notified of material changes via email or through the client portal. Continued use of Oriphim services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
